My Objective of This Project

In my harmonic collection project, I'm going to explore my activity pattern to help me develop a better time management skill.
The goal I have for this project is to find out which activities I have spent time on the most objectively, looking for the reason
why certain kinds of activities took long and what improvement I could implemented to have a balance between each activity
and my daily life.
