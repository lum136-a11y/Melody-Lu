Pros & Cons

As the progress of this project was interrupted because of hospitalization,
I was unable to complete the project effectively, and I realized there were
many points I still needed to improve. This entry is going to provide a balanced
view on the pros and cons I encountered from my experience. How the project
affected my learning and some challenges I faced.

Pros

  • Demonstrate the HTML basis
  • Motivate me to learn time management
  • Get a basic idea of website structure
  • Learn how to publish a website
  • Learn to focus on overall structure
    instead of details
  • Using simple design to convey
    a larger idea
  • Starting from a simple and stupid idea
    Design can take time to improve
  • Take care of my health, taking
    appropriate breaks
  • Don't multitask. Finish one before moving
    on to another.

Cons

  • Skill is not mature enough
  • Often take too much time working
    on each entry
  • Impose high standards on myself,
    underrating what's important
  • Didn't ask for help when needed
  • I gave myself a lot of stress during work
  • The navigation isn't really effective
  • Too much text (should add more visuals)
  • Need more visual elements
  • Can play with more functions
  • Didn't submit the project on time
