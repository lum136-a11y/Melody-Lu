Technique? -- Motivation

In week 10, I focused on the factors that help people persist on a task. Working on a task and spending time waiting for a result to be matured is always exhuasted and discouraging. When this situation happened on me, I often sticked to my phone screen, searching for a temporary space allowed myself to hide from the tedious works that showed no sign of the end.
However, does that solve the problem that I was in emergency of approaching the F range and be unable to complete the work? The answer is No, but also Yes, surprisingly.
Most people rely on time management technique, such as digital calendar to keep their work organized and scheduled. Certainly, time management technique is a helpful source to remind the importance of persistence. However, the true element that drive a person forward is the motivation.
Motivation serves as a guide that leads a person towards a goal. When the goal is clear and concrete, it's easier for people to tolerate the challenges they obstacle.
